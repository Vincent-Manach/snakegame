SNAKE GAME

Veuillez mettre à jour votre navigateur pour accéder au jeu.

Score : 0

Dev by Vincent Manachem, 2020

RULES :

  • Try to feed the snake without hit the side of the map (you would be dead :( )
  • If you eat a candy your snake will get bigger
  • Use the directional arrows of your keyboard to play, the snake will move when you will press one directional arrow
  • You can't turn around, so if you go on the right you can't go on the left direction
  • The space bar can put the game in pause. Press any arrow (exept the reverse arrow : see the previous rule)
